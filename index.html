History

Although we are relatively new directives, we know all the history of UFT and all the 75 years of glory. Who thought we could get this far, as we started on a small village? We were climbing up on the classifications and celebrating promotions back to 1970 because we were the best of the best, and the same applied for many years. We had many football stars in our team, such as Ronaldo, Leo Messi, Neymar and Kun Agüero, all of them with quite a few Golden Balls.

What matters is not we have reached this far because of you. We have won a lot of champions because of you and we have won many promotions and prices because of you.

Context and Actual Situation

Desperate situation

The situation in which we find ourselves is desperate, but not impossible to solve. The latest news indicates that some managers spent the club's money on meals and private parties. At the same time, the revenues continued to decline due to the pandemic.

Irregularities and Controversies

Unfortunately, we also know that there have been suspicious transfers above the current value that have detracted the club’s economy. We have first-hand information that the current president and his management team have been taking money out of our beloved club, such as making unacknowledged payments to player managers, movements in accounts located in tax havens and other illegal irregularities.

Bad player management

Unknown players from foreign countries have been signed for exorbitant amounts of money that will hardly have minutes in an elite team like the United Football Team. In addition, there have been long-term contract renewals for players of a certain age.

Solutions we propose

The solution we propose, as the new managers of the club, will compensate for all the scandals and the bad economical management of the last directives of the club.

Transparent Economy

Everyone will be able to consult our financial actions dedicated to the club via the web.

New Managers for each Department

We will put new managers for each department, for a better organization of the club.

Looking for new Sponsorships

We will start looking for new sponsors and as the pandemic decreases, this will have an impact on the economic growth of the club.

Modernify Players' Contracts to the new Reality

We will review all the players' contracts to adjust them to the new reality that the club is experiencing.
